## Hi, I'm Taylor 👋

I began my career as a Mechanical Engineer, but my desire to serve led me to enlist in the United States Army and pursue a career in Special Forces as a Communications Sergeant (18E). Through my time in Special Operations, I discovered a strong connection between military operational planning and Systems Engineering. Defining mission requirements, developing architectures, managing constraints, integrating capabilities, and validating solutions are all fundamental aspects of both disciplines. This realization opened the door to my passion for Systems Engineering and the opportunity to combine my engineering background with my operational experience.

To further develop my Systems Engineering skillset, I have been working on a personal avionics systems project focused on designing, building, and validating a Raspberry Pi-based rocket flight data acquisition system. The project includes requirements definition, functional analysis, architecture development and allocation, trade studies, verification planning, and prototype testing.
